After moves to various French cities, the squadron reached Gondreville on 24 May 19l8. During its earliest months in France, the 91st Aero Squadron (Observation) engaged in training. They sent a number of their mechanics to the Salmson airplane motor factory for instruction to overcome mechanical and technical
difficulties impending their operations.]
