Recreational Activity in Japan

Page 1 of 1 Page
The flower Sisters performed every other week, at the Yokota Air Base Officer’s Club, and I believe, also, on Friday. There rendition of China Nights has gone around the world. They were the best thing about the Korean Conflict that I know. Michiko was the piano player. Bass fiddle was Mando. Kayolio banjo as was Dori and Minia Yokos. Guitar, J. Yamanota instrument not visible. They were great .

Ctsy. Earl Myers
Yokota AB

Shosaku Mochita

Bill Sutton was a Crew Chief at the Yokota Base Shop. This man,
Shosaku Mochita,
was one of the men he worked with. Bill says that Shosaku was a good friend and a real gentleman. Bill had the occasion to visit him and his family
at their home.

Ctsy. Bill Sutton
