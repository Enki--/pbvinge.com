Introduction

An important purpose of this web site is to bring together the men (and their families and friends) who participated in USAF reconnaissance work in support of WW II, the Korean War and the early Cold War period. We all have stories to tell. Some are short and some are longer. In sorting out the information that comes in to our e-mail address and through the regular mails, it is obvious that there are many short stories that just cry out to be told — stories that will excite and warm the hearts of those who were there and our wider audience, as well. Therefore, this “Contributed Short Stories” chapter is now a part of our web site. Feel free to submit your own stories, long or short. Meaningful stories, that do not contain embarrassing or hurtful content, will be given fair consideration for inclusion.
