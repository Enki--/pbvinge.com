Contributed Okinawan Color and Japanese
Images and Commentary
As our 31/91st SRS web site continues to grow, it is obvious that there
will be pictures and comments accumulated that are not part of a
specific story, but relate to the overall story. The space in this section
will be reserved to present selected photographs and comments
from a variety of sources relating to life on Okinawa and Japan, as
seen through the eyes and camera lenses of the troops working
on the reconnaissance mission in the Far East.
